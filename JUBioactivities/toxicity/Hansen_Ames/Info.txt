Benchmark Data Set
for In Silico Prediction of Ames Mutagenicity

On these pages we present a public benchmark data set of 6512 chemical compounds together with their Ames mutagenicity
test results (2-class classification problem). The data set was designed for the evaluation of in silico prediction methods

Publication:

J. Chem. Inf. Model. 2009, 49, 2077–2081
Benchmark Data Set for in Silico Prediction of Ames Mutagenicity
Katja Hansen, Sebastian Mika, Timon Schroeter, Andreas Sutter, Antonius ter Laak,
Thomas Steger-Hartmann, Nikolaus Heinrich, and Klaus-Robert Müller
